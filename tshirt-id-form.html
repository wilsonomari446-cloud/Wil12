FAQ About Distribution

How long does distribution take?

After submitting the form, we'll contact you within 24-48 hours with pickup/delivery arrangements. Distribution typically happens within 3-5 business days.

Can I pick up my items?

Yes! We offer both pickup and delivery options. Once we receive your form, we'll discuss the best option with you.

What if I submitted this form before?

You can resubmit if you haven't been contacted yet. Just fill out the form again and mention in the notes section that this is a resubmission.

What if my form isn't loading?

Try refreshing the page. If issues persist, contact us directly via WhatsApp at +255 612 801 249 or email wilsonomari446@gmail.com

Can first and second years both submit?

Yes! This form is open to all students including first years, second years, and beyond. Just select your year from the dropdown.

Is my information secure?

Yes. We use Google Forms which is secure and your information will only be used for T-Shirt and ID distribution purposes.
